Khulnasoft has implemented a blackhole MX server you can use with Transactional Email, to prevent your emails from being rejected by recipient servers. This happens when you have no MX record or when you have configured a null MX record. [Find out how to configure Khulnasoft's MX.](/managed-services/transactional-email/how-to/add-mx-record-to-your-domain/)
