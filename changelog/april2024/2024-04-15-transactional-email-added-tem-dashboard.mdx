We are excited to announce that you can now view your Transactional Email metrics in a managed dashboard in Grafana! Available through the Cockpit product, you can effortlessly monitor your email performance and the reputation of your domains.

Access your dashboard via your domain's **Metrics** tab in the [Khulnasoft console.](https://console.khulnasoft.com/transactional-email/domains/)
